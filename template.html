4P Analysis

• Toyota's 4P Analysis (Product, Price, Place, Promotion) to organize business strategy
• Focus on differentiation in specialized applications and global market recognition improvement

Product

  • Hybrid technology providing efficient and environmentally friendly lineup, emphasizing technological innovation
  • Diverse vehicle range including SUVs, sedans, and trucks to serve broad customer segments
  • Market expansion through not only new vehicles but also used car sales and certified pre-owned programs

Place

  • Strengthen global dealer network covering urban to rural areas
  • Enhance customer convenience by combining online sales with click & collect models
  • Improve post-purchase customer satisfaction through comprehensive service centers and customer support

Price

  • Competitive pricing to secure market share while adding premium through technological differentiation
  • Regional price adjustments based on local purchasing power for effective market adaptation
  • Expand customer base by reducing purchase barriers through lease contracts and installment plans

Promotion

  • Enhance brand image through sponsorships of sports events and cultural programs
  • Emphasize reliability and sustainability through advertising campaigns linked to environmental responsibility
  • Improve recognition among younger demographics using targeted social media campaigns
